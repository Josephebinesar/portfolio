About Me

My journey into Artificial Intelligence

I'm Joseph Ebinesar, a final-year Computer Science Engineering student at Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science, Chennai. My journey into AI began with a simple question: "Can machines truly understand humans?"

That curiosity transformed into a passion. I built FeelUp, an AI-powered mental health platform using transformer embeddings, semantic peer matching, and privacy-first architecture. My research on FeelUp achieved Precision@5: 0.867 and sub-second response times.

I've competed in hackathons like Infosys Hackathon, Google Thinkathon, SRM Hackathon, and Saveetha Innovation Challenge, and presented research at academic conferences. I also run Tech With Ebi on YouTube — simplifying AI for everyone.

FeelUp: AI Powered Emotion Sharing and Support Platform

Authors: Manya N V A Joseph Ebinesar Vasantharaj K

An AI-powered emotional support platform using transformer-based sentence embeddings for semantic understanding of user emotions, ANN peer matching via pgvector, and a privacy-first architecture using PostgreSQL Row-Level Security. Achieves Precision@5 of 0.867 with sub-second latency.
